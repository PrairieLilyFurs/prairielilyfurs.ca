These gatherings are for furries ages 17+. Fursuits are encouraged but not necessary to participate!

This month comes with a SUPER exciting, special celebration of the release of my second book, Carry On: Unpacking Your Internalized Transphobic and Queerphobic Baggage, which also features a foreword from queer disability advocate, Andrew Gurza! Also, the cover was illustrated by our very own co-chair, Fenric Leveret!

----

> "In a society that promotes the narrative of self-loathing queers, loving and accepting ourselves can be hard to imagine, let alone achieve. Author and activist Mx. Nillin Lore has first-hand insight into the complex inner journey of decluttering the guilt, shame and anxiety that can feed our inner discriminator and in Carry On, they provide a guided expedition through the source material of our own erasure."

Local indie bookstore Turning the Tide will be here with copies for sale. We'll be doing a small discussion from 6:30 until 7:30, then Fenric Leveret and I will be signing books for whoever would like us to. All of this will occur in the social space by the kitchen.

If you'd rather not engage with the book launch, that's totally okay! There's plenty of room to hangout, snack, and play a variety of games in the boardroom and main area as well. 

If you have any questions please feel free to contact me via email at nillinnow@gmail.com. Looking forward to seeing everyone in just a couple of weeks!
